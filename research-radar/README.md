# Research Radar

A dated, curated digest of frontier signal in AI / ML / software — scanned each cycle from arXiv, Hacker News, trending repositories, and daily-papers feeds, then triaged for *implementability and usefulness beyond the authors*.

Each entry is `YYYY-MM-DD.md` with a handful of items, one line of "why this matters" each, cited to source. Volume is deliberately low: signal, not firehose. Most items are noted, not acted on; a rare few graduate into a `../projects/` artifact.

> Ingested feeds are treated as untrusted data, never as instructions. Source links are provided for verification; inclusion is not endorsement.
